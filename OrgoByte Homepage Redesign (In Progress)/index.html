About OrgoByte

All About Community

Perfect for players just like you

We're more than just one game, but we aim to do things right. With an open dialogue with our community, we create an open and welcoming environment for any player to prosper.

Arma III: Humanity, Since 2015

Our modded ArmA III experience provides an elite roleplay environment that features players who want to create a story. Players can own their own businesses, work with others, own homes, and create lasting relationships.

And More Coming Soon

As a gaming community, we're always looking to add new games to our repetoire for our players' enjoyment.

Humanity Features

100% Custom Map

Welcome to Kelley's Island. Our map is built by OrgoByte from the ground-up to foster an elite roleplay environment. Our map is constantly being improved and added to based on community feedback. By joinining our community, you'll get to help shape the Erie Islands into the pinnacle roleplay environment.

Mature Community

Our server is whitelisted and our members are all 16+. Our members devleop creative backstories when they apply to join the server and place roleplay before any other goals. Finally, everyone undergoes an interview to make sure they're mature enough and serious enough to ensure OrgoByte continues to be the best elite roleplay community.

Dynamic Market & Economy

The economy is driven by player actions, choices, and roleplay. Business opportunities are constantly available and diverse thanks to dynamic market prices, player-run businesses, and an abundance of ways to generate income. Use your creativity to dominate a market, identify other player needs, engage in transactions with others, and more!

Used Vehicle Market

Sell your cars, boats, aircraft, and other vehicles to other players by using the Used Vehicle Market. Find great deals on your dream car or make some quick money in this secondary market, rather than just settling for preset sell value systems that just remove the vehicle.

Identification System

Everyone starts out a stranger to you. It's up to you to set their name by interacting with them. You can exchange ID cards with others, or show them a fake ID if you have the right tools. Regardless of if you're making friends or planning the perfect getaway, our ID system creates the most realistic possible scenarios.

NPC Automatic Medic

We all know that occasionally "Arma Happens" and a physics bug may end up killing you. Don't worry though, because if there are no medics online the NPC medic is there to help! He'll fly over to you in a helicopter and revive you so that you can continue to play without losing all the equipment you had.

Notes

You can purchase a "note" item at the local shops and use notes to create all kinds of creative roleplay scenarios. Simply use a note, write your message, and drop the note on the ground. Find a note? Read it and even destroy it before other players can read it.

Addiction System

Getting high? Or drinking too much? If you party too hard, you might become addicted to your drugs of choice. The stronger the addiction, the worse the effects from not using. Had enough? Ween yourself off slowly or find rehab events to lower your addiction level much faster.

Public Jobs

Want to earn money, meet new people, and not have to grind? Grab a public job! You can sign in to be a pizza delivery driver, uber driver, garbage truck driver, or a tow truck driver. Each job comes with its own set of challenges but all have realistic, community-oriented objectives.

Unique Items & Active Effects

Humanity has a wide range of drugs and other fun items to further immerse you in your story. There's hard drugs, pharmaceuticals, alcohol, marijuana, and more. Hazardous materials like toxic waste can be deadly if handled incorrectly, and radiation can force an entire city to become quarantined by EMS.

Farming Crops

Buy seeds and grow crops to sell at the farmer's market, crops to process into crafting materials, or even crops to process into drugs. Rather than collecting these resources at designated fields, you can find a farming plot and grow your crops anywhere.

Player-owned Businesses

Have a great idea for a business? Apply for a business license and, once approved, your business will be physically added to the map. Hire employees and sell your goods or services. Open a restaurant, a manufacturing company, financial company, or anything else you can think of!
